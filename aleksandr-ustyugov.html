How Much Is The Net Worth Of Aleksandr Ustyugov?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Aleksandr Ustyugov is a Russian actor widely known for his appearance in Mentovskie voyny series,  Netflix's Better Than Us, and many others. 

Ustyugov has also directed the movie Sluzhu Sovetskomu Soyuzu! (2012) and TV series Gosudarstvennaya zashchita.

Aleksandr Ustyugov Early Life And Education

Aleksandr Ustyugov, aka Aleksandr Sergeevich Ustyugov, was born on 17 October 1976 in Ekibastuz, Kazakhstan, USSR. He was born to parents: Olga Rudolfovna (mother) and Roman Valertevich Ustyugov (father). 

As of 2021, Ustyugov is 44 years old. He holds a Russian nationality and belongs to a mixed ethnic background. 

Regarding his academic qualification, Aleksandr attended Ekibastuz secondary school. Following his graduation, he went to a vocational school specializing in Electrical Repair, Installation, and Dismantling of Mining Equipment. 

After graduation, he worked in Vostochny Coal Mine. In around 1988, he joined an art school where Ustyugov also practiced boxing. He got a degree in acting in 1993. 

Aleksandr Ustyugov went to the Omsk State Transport University in 1993. He transferred to Omsk Youth Theatre after three-semester. He officially entered the theatre industry in 1996.

Better Than Us Actor Aleksandr Ustyugov's Journey: Career Highlights 

In 1996, Aleksandr Ustyugov went to the Omsk Regional College of Culture and Art. After 3 years, he entered the Boris Shchukin Theatre Institute and graduated from there in 2002. He is also a graduate of the Higher Theatre School, Moscow. 

Aleksandr appeared in several plays while he was still studying. Later in 2002, he got his first movie role in the film The Star. 

In 2005, he got a major role in the TV series Adyutanty lyubvi as Platon Tolstoy for a total of 80 episodes. The same year, he landed a role in the TV series Mentovskie voyny as Roman Shilov. Furthermore, he appeared in the TV series for 5 seasons. 

His other notable works are in movies and TV series Viking, Panfilvo's 28, Sled Tigra, The Golden Horde, Rikoshet, and others. 

Mentovskie voyny actor recently appeared in Netflix's TV series Better Than Us as Victor Pavlovich Tropov aka Victor Bot. He performed alongside Paulina Andreeva, Kirill Käro, Olga Lomonosova, Eldar Kalimulin, and other co-stars. 

Apart from his contribution to the film industry, Aleksandr Ustyugov has also contributed to the music industry. Back in 2015, he established his own band named Ekibastuz. 

How Much Is The Net Worth Of Aleksandr Ustyugov? 

As of 2021, Aleksandr Ustyugov is estimated to have a net worth of $1 million, according to several sources. He mainly earns from his professional career as a TV actor and theater artist. 

In Russia, an actor's average income is 1,370,419 ₽ ($11.6 thousand) per year. 

Besides that. he also adds a reasonable sum of money from the album sales and performances. 

Is Aleksandr Ustyugov Married? Personal Life

Aleksandr Ustyugov is a divorcee. He is married twice.

Back in 2005, Ustyugov exchanged his marital vows with Yanina Sokolovskaya on 6 August 2005. The ex-couple share a child together. Their daughter's name is Evgeniya Ustyugov, born on 9 November 2007. 

The love birds were together for a decade and separated in 2015 with mutual understanding. 

The same year, Aleksandr walked down the aisle with Anna Ozar on 23 September 2015. But their marriage only lasted 3 months. 

Currently, Aleksandr Ustyugov is reported to be living a single life.
