Big Sean Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 1, 2024

Who is Big Sean?

Big Sean is an American rapper from Detroit. He is professionally known as Big Sean. He released his debut studio album, ‘Finally Famous’ in 2011.

Big Sean’s Early Life, Childhood, and Education

Sean was born in Santa Monica, California on March 25, 1988, to parents Myra and James Anderson.

When he was 3 months old, he moved to Detroit, Michigan, where he was raised by his mother, a school teacher, and grandmother. He is of American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to the African-American nationality.

Talking about his education, Sean graduated from Cass Technical High School with a 3.7 GPA.

Big Sean’s Career, Salary, and Net Worth

Sean initially gained a valuable relationship with Detroit hip-hop station WHTD. Later, he freestyled for Kanye West in 2005.  Eventually, West signed Big Sean to GOOD Music.

He released his first official mixtape ‘Finally Famous: The Mixtape’ on September 30, 2007. Additionally, he released a second mixtape hosted by Mick Boogie on April 16, 2009, called ‘UKNOWBIGSEAN’.

Sean released his debut studio album ‘Finally Famous’ on June 28, 2011.  The album featured guest appearances from Lupe Fiasco, John Legend, Pharrell, Kanye West, Roscoe Dash, Wiz Khalifa, Chiddy Bang, Rick Ross, and Nicki Minaj among others.

On September 5, 2012, Big Sean released the mixtape ‘Detroit’.

He released his second album ‘Hall of Fame on August 27, 2013. Some of the tours that Sean has been part of are the I Am Finally Famous Tour, Detroit Tour, Hall of Fame Tour and I Decided Tour.

Furthermore, some other studio albums that he has released are ‘Dark Sky Paradise, ‘Twenty88’, ‘I Decided’ and ‘Double or Nothing.

Sean won the BET Awards in 2012 in the category of Best Male Hip-Hop Artist. Additionally, he has also won BET Hip Hop Awards, MTV Video Music Awards, Soul Train Music Awards, and YouTube Music Awards.

Furthermore, he has garnered the nomination for awards like Billboard Music Awards, Grammy Awards, and iHeartRadio Music Awards among others.

Big Sean and Rapper and producer Hit-Boy collaborated to release the EP What You Expect in October 2021.

Hate Our Love, a song by Big Sean and Queen Naija was released in February 2022.

Sean has not revealed his current salary. However, he has an estimated net worth of around $26 million at present.

Is Big Sean involved in any rumors and controversies?

Sean became part of a controversy after he was arrested for third-degree sexual assault at a concert in Lewiston, New York. On October 26, he pleaded guilty to second-degree unlawful imprisonment and was fined $750.

Furthermore, he has also been criticized for the nature of his lyrics several times. At present, there are no rumors concerning his life and career.

Big Sean’s Body Measurement

Talking about his body measurement, Sean has a height of 5 feet 8 inches or 173 cm. Additionally, he weighs around 69 kg or 152 pounds.

Furthermore, his hair color is black and his eye color is dark brown.

Big Sean’s Social Media

Sean is active on social media. He has a huge number of followers on social networking sites such as Facebook, Twitter as well as Instagram.

He has more than 14.4M followers on Twitter. In addition, he has more than 13.2M followers on Instagram. Similarly, his Facebook page has more than 7.6M followers.
