Facts About the Chef  Milena Broger

The Austrian cook Milena Broger, who has received numerous prizes in her industry and has a solid reputation, is not yet featured on Wikipedia despite working in the field and having a solid reputation.

She and her life partner Erik Pedersen are currently in Weiss, a nearly century-old former coffee shop. The Weiss restaurant regularly posts visually beautiful photos of their food on Instagram, including dishes that are part of their limited-time specials. They are constantly looking to hire a modest number of new staff members for their restaurant.

Milena Broger: Who Is She?

Milena Broger, an Austrian native born in 1992, is 30 years old.

She has chosen to focus on cooking since it helps her get in touch with her own self. Broger contends that eating should both replenish the body and the intellect.

The Weiss, a nearly century-old former coffee house and inn in Bregenz’s Anton-Schneider-Strasse 5, is where Milena Broger, Austria’s youngest female chef with a toque from Gault&Millau, honed her skills. The young cook and her partner Erik Pedersen pleased the restaurant’s manager Theresa Feuerstein both in the kitchen and outside it. They moved to Vorarlberg after her persuasion.

The mountains, human contact, and the feeling of intimacy in her life, according to the woman who has a profound appreciation for nature, are her favorites. Milena Broger enjoys going for walks in the woods. She frequently does this near or far from her Bregenz home, as in the direction of Pfander or the Lecknertal valley.

She also likes taking hikes to the Sonderdach, which is above Bezau and is where her father spent his formative years.
