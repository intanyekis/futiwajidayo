James Harden &amp; Khloe Kardashians Baby Plans Before Marriage Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 13, 2024

“James is in love with Khloe and is really all in with the relationship with her, as well,” an insider tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “He really believes they will be married, but he doesn’t want to pop the question because she is apprehensive about marriage with the fallout from Lamar [Odom]. But they have definitely talked about babies and they want one with each other. But Khloe knows that James wants an NBA Championship first. that’s his first priority.”

Wow! Khloe and James — whose jersey you can buy here — only started dating a few months ago, so it’s pretty crazy that things are already this serious. However, we can completely understand why she’d be apprehensive about tying the knot so quickly again, especially considering how messy her divorce from Lamar has been.

As HollywoodLife.com previously reported, James is really into the reality star, and has even started telling his close friends that “she’s the one!” Even Khloe’s BFF, Malika Haqq, revealed that Khloe’s “really happy” in her new relationship. And she’s proven it to James, too, by throwing him a lavish yacht party for his 26th birthday over the summer. NO BIG DEAL!

Meanwhile, one person whose not happy about this new relationship is Khloe’s ex. “She’s [my] soulmate,” Lamar told InTouch. “I can’t control it. We’ll never part. I would never consider myself serious with another woman. To sum it up, if a man is attempting to court Khloe, he’s probably wasting his time.” Uh…it sure doesn’t seem like it to us!
