American actress Kaitlin Olson has an estimated net worth of $6 million in 2013. Olson is best recognized for her appearance on the TV series It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia where she played the role of Deandra “Sweet Dee” Reynolds.

Kaitlin Olson was born on August 18, 1975 in Tigard, Oregon, U.S. Since 2008, she has been married to Rob McElhenney with whom she has two children named Axel Lee and Leo Grey. After studying Theater from the University of Oregon, Olson moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career on acting.

Debuting in the industry in 2000, Olson has already appeared on a couple of film and TV projects such as Eyes to Heaven, Jacks, Coyote Ugly, Meet the Marks, Fugly, Curb Your Enthusiasm, The Drew Carey Show, Scapegoats, Miss Match, Significant Others, George Lopez, Kelsey Grammer Presents: The Sketch Show, It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia, The Riches, Out of Practice, Weather Girl, Leap Year, Held Up, Family Guy, Unsupervised, Brickleberry, and The Heat.
