Marquise Derrell Goodwin is an American football wide receiver and kicks returner for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League (NFL) who was born on  November 19, 1990, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Marquise is an Olympian who competed in the long jump in track and field. He was drafted by the Buffalo Bills in the third round with the 78th pick of the 2013 NFL Draft. He played college football in Texas.
