What are the birth facts of Mozart La Para?

Mozart La Para was born Erickson Fernández on the 31st of January 1988 in Los Mina, Santo Domingo. He is of Spanish ancestry.

He is the son of Rafael Fernandez and Mélida Ferreira.

He has a younger sister, Katherine.
