American rapper Papoose came into prominence for his 1999 single Alphabetical Slaughter.

Along with a successful career, the fandom is well curious about the star's happy married life with his beautiful rapper wife.

However, he failed to create similar success for the longest time. Finally, the star's debut album, The Nacirema Dream, which was released in 2013, became successful. It recovered positive reviews and sold 5,442 copies in the first week. 

Apart from his music career, the star is also known for his appearance on the VH1 reality series Love & Hip Hop: New York.

Papoose's Married Life with Wife

The star is married to rapper and Remy Ma.

The couple was initially scheduled to get married on 27th April of 2008.

But their life turned upside down when Remy was convicted of assault, illegal weapon possession, and attempted coercion and sentenced to eight years jail sentence in 2007. 

Papoose with wife Remy Ma on 15th February 2020 (Photo: Instagram)

Even with the jail bars between them, the couple got married on 12th May 2008 over the phone. During Remy's time in jail, her husband stood by her side; he made sure to visit her regularly throughout her first year in the prison until Remy herself asked him not to. 

Later, the couple held the wedding ceremony in 2016, after Remy's release from prison.

Further, on the occasion of their 10th wedding anniversary in 2018, the star and Remy renewed their wedding vows in an intimate ceremony in front of their closest friends and family members.
