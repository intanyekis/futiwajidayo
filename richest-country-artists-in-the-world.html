10 Luke Bryan

Net Worth: $160 Million

Luke Bryan's career began in the early 2000s, and he has since become one of the most popular country music artists in the world.

Bryan has released twelve studio albums, which produced 30 number-one singles on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.

He has also won numerous awards, including five Academy of Country Music Awards, two Country Music Association Awards, and six American Music Awards.

Having sold over 75 million records, Luke Bryan has a net worth of a whopping $170 million.

Bryan's touring revenue is particularly significant. His 2014 Dirt Road Tour presented 72 shows, and pulled in over $61 million in revenue.

Net Worth: $190 Million

With over 30 million albums sold worldwide and 32 number-one hits on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart, Kenny Chesney is a titan of the country music genre.

His talent caught the attention of Capricorn Records, which released his debut album, In My Wildest Dreams, in 1994. Chesney's breakthrough came with his sophomore album All I Need To Know in 1995.

Chesney has received twelve Country Music Association Awards (including the Country Music Association Award for Entertainer of the Year honor four times), and eleven Academy of Country Music Awards (including four consecutive Academy of Country Music Awards for Entertainer of the Year from 2005 to 2008), as well as six Grammy Award nominations.

His 2007 Flip-Flop Summer Tour was the highest-grossing country road trip of the year, second only to that of The Police. Most of Chesney's net worth was generated through his tours and concerts.

7 Faith Hill

Net Worth: $200 Million

Faith Hill is one of the most successful country music artists of all time, having sold almost 50 million albums worldwide.

She is critically acclaimed in the industry, having won numerous awards, including five Grammy Awards, six American Music Awards, and fourteen Country Music Association Awards.

Hill was signed to Warner/Reprise Records in 1993 and released her debut album, Take Me As I Am, the same year. In 2001, Hill recorded There You'll Be for the Pearl Harbor soundtrack, which was commercially successful; it remains her best-selling single in Europe.

Hill has also seen success in her entrepreneurial ventures. She has her own clothing line, perfume line, and production company.

In 2009, Hill released her first fragrance, Faith Hill Parfums. Her net worth is estimated to be around $200 million.

Hill earns money through record sales, touring, merchandising, endorsements, and songwriting royalties, and she is one of the highest-paid country music artists in the world.

5 George Strait

Net Worth: $300 Million

George Strait's music career kicked off during his college days.

He has won numerous awards, including 13 Academy of Country Music Awards and 22 Country Music Association Awards.

Strait has been certified as the twelfth best-selling artist in American history, with career record sales of 70 million in the United States.

Strait's touring career is a revenue generating machine; his tours earned him $99 million in three years.

He is credited with designing a 360-degree configuration, which introduced more festive-style tours.

The Grammy Award-winning artist toured for the last time in 2012; the tour was immensely successful and set a new record for the highest-grossing single-show concert, with earnings of over $18 million in a single performance.

3 Shania Twain

Net Worth: $400 Million

Shania Twain's musical career has been immensely lucrative, marked by the sale of over 100 million records.

She is one of the best-selling music artists of all time, and the best-selling female artist in country music history. Twain has won numerous awards, including five Grammy Awards and 27 American Music Awards.

Twain began singing in bars and clubs to support herself after her parents died in a car accident. Her debut album was unsuccessful, but her sophomore album sold over $18 million copies and gained her notoriety.

The Queen of Country Pop released her third album, Come On Over, and went on a global tour. Deemed one of the most anticipated of the 1990s, the tour became the highest-grossing tour by a female country artist at the time.

In the end, Twain drew over two million spectators, and earned over $80 million. She has diversified her portfolio with several successful business ventures, significantly bolstering her substantial net worth.

Twain is the highest-grossing female country touring artist, with $421 million grossed from her concert tours alone.

In his impressive career, Garth Brooks has achieved nine diamond-certified albums, the only artist in any genre to achieve this feat.

Brooks's touring revenue is particularly significant. The Garth Brooks Stadium Tour launched in March 2019 and broke attendance records in 15 cities, attracting nearly 1.2 million fans and generating over $97 million in gross revenue.
