Alessandro Nesta is a former Italian professional football player with an estimated net worth of $2.3 million.

Alessandro Nesta started his career when he joined Lazio's youth academy in 1985. Nine years later, he debuted to the team's Serie A. He was selected to become the recipient of the Serie A Young Footballer of the Year Award in 1998 and was a four-time Serie A Defender of the Year awardee.

In 2002-2003 season, he transferred to Milan and spent 10 seasons with the team. Nesta did 325 caps and scored 10 goals. He signed a contract with the Montreal Impact in 2012. Two years later, he joined the Indian Super League's Chennaiyin FC.

Alessandro Nesta was born on March 19, 1976 in Rome, Italy.
