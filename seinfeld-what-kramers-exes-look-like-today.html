What Kramer's Exes From The Show Look Like Today

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Although Kramer wasn't a great romantic, he still had a thriving dating life throughout Seinfeld's run. Cosmo Kramer was the wacky neighbor who often involved himself in Jerry, Elaine, and George's adventures.

Although Kramer never has a long-term relationship, he is involved with many women over the nine seasons of Seinfeld. It usually goes wrong, and his three friends do not usually help him. Although he never dates Elaine, many people felt they should have ended up together.

Here are some Kramer's exes and what they look like today.

12 Donna Evans Merlo As Handicapped Lola

Lola is severely injured after crashing her wheelchair into a wall because George's father has parked in the handicapped spot. Kramer replaces her wheelchair and falls in love with her. She dumps him for not being good-looking enough for her. She appears in the finale, testifying against Kramer and George.

Merlo is better known for her stunt work. She has worked stunt double for Gwyneth Paltrow in Iron Man and Iron Man 2 and a utility stunts performer in The Avengers. Seinfeld is one of her only acting credits.

11 Ashley Gardner As Poetry-Writing Librarian Marion

On a trip to the library, Kramer meets Marion who he believes is a lonely woman desperately seeking companionship. As delusional as ever, Kramer thinks she needs him in her life. Fans mainly remember her of the show as the woman whose poetry makes Kramer cry.

Seinfeld was Gardner's first TV credit in 1991. She is best known for voicing Nancy and Didi in King Of The Hill, but she appeared in a number of high-profile TV shows including Reno 911! ER, and Six Feet Under. More recently, she has offered additional voices for Beavis and Butt-Head, Tarantula and WTF 101.

10 Anita Barone As Snub-Loving Gail Cunningham

Anita Barone dates both Jerry and Kramer. She first meets Gail after witnessing her refusing to kiss Jerry goodnight after a date. Despite snubbing her, the second they meet, they kiss. They believe that she likes being snubbed.

The American actress starred on The Jeff Foxworthy Show for the first season and appeared in The War at Home from 2005 to 2007. She was noticeably the first Carol in Friends, before the role was recast. More recently, Barone is known for her recurring role in the Disney Channel sitcom Shake It Up as Officer Georgia Jones, the mother of Bella Thorne's character CeCe.

9 Lisa Arch As Mobster Connie Who Tries To Kill Kramer

In season 7's "The Friar's Club," Connie was Kramer's mobster girlfriend. Connie noticeably called Kramer by his first name and at one point she thought he had died on top of her. She calls her mobster friend who throws a sleeping Kramer into the Hudson. He wakes up floating in the East River in a sack. She was ultimately arrested for trying to kill Kramer.

The comedian is now known for her recurring role on Curb Your Enthusiasm as Cassie, the wife of Larry's cousin. She also appeared on Mad TV and replaced Annabelle Gurwitch as the hostess of the TBS cooking show, Dinner and a Movie. Her most recent role is as the principal in Pretty Freekin Scary.

8 Wendie Malick As Wendy With The Polarizing Haircut

The relationship that started and ended with a haircut. Wendy is Elaine's physical therapist friend on Seinfeld who dates Kramer for a short period of time. Elaine hates her haircut and hopes Kramer will insult it. Instead, he compliments her and the pair date. Much to Elaine's delight, he dumps her when she cuts her hair.

Malick is hugely successful and has made over 50 guest appearances on television shows. She appears in Shrinking alongside Harrison Ford, played Geraldine in Dear White People, and President Hagemeyer in Young Sheldon.

7 Lisa Houle As Cheryl The Chemist

Kramer dates Cheryl, who dates a chemist just to influence test results. Kramer has invested in a non-fat yogurt place, and although business is booming, people are complaining about their weight gain. Jerry and Elaine take a sample to the lab to test the fat content, and Kramer dates Cheryl to falsify the verdict. It's one of the few times Kramer actually comes across as a smooth talker.

Houle has had limited acting credits since her role in Seinfeld. Her most recent credit is 2019's Dreamland alongside Juliette Lewis. She married Stephen McHattie, with whom she has three children.

6 Wendel Meldrum As Leslie The Low Talker

Leslie appears in The Puffy Shirt as Kramer's date, with whom Jerry complains is a low talker. Jerry accidentally agrees to wear a piece from the clothing designer on The Today Show. He tries to back out when he discovers it's a puffy pirate shirt. Leslie also makes a brief appearance in the finale, taking the stand to testify against the "New York Four".

Wendel Meldrum won multiple accolades for her role of Anne on Less Than Kind. In the 90s she had leading roles in The Wonder Years and Pursuit Of Happiness. Her later roles were smaller, making guest appearances in NYPD Blue, Saving Hope, and Working The Engels. She sadly passed away in 2021.

5 Sunday Theodore As Olive The Cashier

Olive is a cashier at Monk's Caf√©, mostly remembered for her long fingernails in Seinfeld. They meet in The Pie when Kramer asks Elaine to scratch his back and Olive offers to do it inside. Kramer is accused of cheating on her with a mannequin that resembles Elaine.

Sunday Theodore's credits are few and far between since appearing on Seinfeld. Her last credit was in Cuba Gooding Jr's film Bayou Caviar. Outside of her acting credits she works as a concierge in hotels.

4 Kim Myers As The Pam The Bookstore Manager

Pam is the bookstore manager who gets caught in a lover's triangle with Jerry and Kramer. Appearing in episode 8's The Soul Mate, Kramer works with Newman to steal Pam from Jerry. The pair fight over her until she reveals she doesn't want children. Both Kramer and Jerry decide to get a vasectomy to impress Pam, but Jerry backs out while Kramer goes through with the operation.

Kim Myers is a popular scream queen, appearing in A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge and 1996's Hellraiser: Bloodline. In recent years she has had minor roles in The Closer, Judging Amy and Six Feet Under.

3 Kelly Coffield As Noreen

Noreen is Elaine's friend on Seinfeld, who she works hard to try to destroy. Kramer takes pity on her, and they end up dating. She later announces she is pregnant, which she isn't happy about. It's never revealed if she has their child or not.

The comedian and actress has appeared in numerous guest roles across the last two decades including playing a judge in How To Get Away With Murder and The Good Wife, starring The Young And The Restless and 30 Rock.

2 Debi A Monahan As The Murdered Actress

Chelsea Lang is an aspiring actress Kramer started dating while living in Los Angeles. They meet during an audition for a horror movie, and she talks about her agent trying to land her the role of Eva Braun. The Smog Strangler later murders Chelsea, and while he claims not to be bothered, he clearly is upset by her death.

Her last acting credit was in a 2003 episode of The Practice. Other credits to her name include minor roles in Stark Trek: Voyager and Party Of Five. Monahan currently lives in California and works as a celebrity Pilates and fitness instructor, helping to sculpt Victoria's Secret Angel's bodies.

1 Sarah Silverman As "Jimmy Legs" Emily

Sarah Silverman appears as Emily in The Money. She has "jimmy legs" which keep Kramer awake throughout their relationship. The issue is fixed when they start sleeping in the Costanza's twin beds.

Silverman is a hugely successful actress and comedian. She starred in and produced The Sarah Silverman Program, for which she was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Comedy Series. She is also a political activist and a popular podcaster.
