Slava Voynov is a Russian professional ice hockey which has an estimated net worth of $4.1 million.

Slava Voynov started his career in Russia when he joined the Russian Superleague's Traktor Chelyabinsk in 2006-2007 season. Upon completing his second campaign with the team, he was selected by the Los Angeles Kings during the 2008 NHL Entry Draft.

When he decided to forego the junior process in the United States, he went on to play for Kings' affiliate, Manchester Monarchs. Voynov set a new record for the Kings during the 2011-2012 season playoffs for the number of goals scored by a defender.
