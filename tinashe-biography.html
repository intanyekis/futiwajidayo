Tinashe Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 1, 2024

Who is Tinashe?

Tinashe is a popular American singer and songwriter best known for her debut single ‘2 On’.

Likely, her notable roles included as a motion-capture model in the animated film The Polar Express (2004), Robin Wheeler in Out of Jimmy’s Head (2007–2008), and a recurring role in the CBS series Two and a Half Men (2008–2009).

Tinashe: Age, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity

She was born in Lexington, Kentucky, the United States on February 6, 1993. Her birth name is Tinashe Jorgensen Kachingwe. Her father’s name is Michael Kachingwe and her mother’s name is Aimie Kachingwe.

While her father taught acting, her mother was a teacher of physical therapy at California State University.

She has two siblings namely, Thulani Kachingwe and Kudzai Kachingwe. She holds American citizenship and mixed (Zimbabwean- Irish- Norwegian- Danish- English- Scottish) ethnicity. Her birth sign is Aquarius.

In which school did Tinashe attend?

Talking about her education history, she attended Crescenta Valley High School.

Tinashe: Professional Life, Career

Talking about Tinashe’s profession, in 2007, Tinashe embarked on her career. She found herself a place in the girl band, ‘The Stunners’ alongside Marisol Esparza, Allie Gonino, Hayley Kiyoko, and Kelsey Sanders.

They’re recorded single initially made for the ‘iCarly’ soundtrack was never released. In the year 2009, ‘The Stunners’ left Columbia Records and instead signed a production deal with Lionsgate Entertainment.

Immediately thereafter, in March, the group released a single ‘Bubblegum’ on iTunes. Later in October, they released a 5-song EP influenced by artists such as Madonna, Gwen Stefani, and Rihanna.

The group’s first-ever music video for their promo single, ‘We Got It’ was released in 2010. Right after its release, they performed the song on The Today Show and The Wendy Williams Show.

In 2011, The Stunners officially disbanded. Following this, Tinashe embarked on a solo career.

Whereas, her first-ever solo televised performance was for the Dodgers-Cubs game wherein she sang ‘God Bless America’.

Tinashe’s first solo music video was released in June 2011. It was a cover of Lil Wayne’s single ‘How to Love’. She soon followed it up with the music video for her first original solo song ‘Can’t Say No’.

Following the success of her first two ventures, Tinashe came up with her third mixtape ‘Black Water’. Comprising of thirteen tracks, the mixtape did exceedingly well and was declared a hit.

The single ‘Vulnerable’ featuring rapper Travis Scott was declared a ‘Must-Hear Pop Song of the Week’ by MTV’s Buzzworthy. In the year 2014, she started working on her debut studio album ‘Aquarius’.

On January 13, she released her first single from her debut album, ‘2 On’. She soon followed it up with her second single ‘Pretend’ featuring ASAP Rocky. Upon the release of her debut album ‘Aquarius’.

Though Tinashe has made a strong foothold in music, her acting career isn’t far behind. Not many know that Tinashe first started as a baby model and had a prominent acting career before turning to music, her first love.

Tinashe had made her film debut in 2003 with ‘Masked and Anonymous as Mrs. Brown’s daughter. Three years before this, she had done a television film ‘Cora Unashamed’.

She even appeared in the 2006 American drama film ‘Akeelah and the Bee. Whereas, from 2008 to 2009, Tinashe had a recurring role in the American sitcom ‘Two and a Half Men as Celeste.

Awards, Nomination 

She won Best Young Ensemble Performance in a TV Series for Out of Jimmy’s Head (2007) at the Young Artist Award.
