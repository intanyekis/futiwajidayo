Who Is Michael Bolton Married To, Who Is Michael Boltons Wife?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Who Is Michael Bolton?

Michael Bolton is a singer and songwriter from America. He gave his performance in the heavy metal and hard rock genres from the 1970s to the 1980s. He was well-known for his series of pop-rock ballads, which were recorded after a stylist change in the late 1980s. He sold above 75 million records, which is considered to be an achievement in his history.  He recorded top 10 albums and two number-one singles on the Billboard charts. He won six American music awards and two Grammy Awards.

Who Is Michael Bolton Married To?

Michael Bolton is single now. He is not married to anyone now. He was married to Maureen McGuire from 1975 to 1990. Michael and Maureen are the parents of the three daughters. They are Holly, Isa, and Taryn. He had become a grandfather to his six grandchildren in 2019. He got an introduction to actress Nicollete Sheridan in the year 1992. After that, Bolton dated Sheridan till 1995. They were united again and confirmed their engagement in March 2006. Later in August 2008, they announced that they had split by breaking their engagement.

Michael Bolton Biography

The real name of Michael Bolton is Michael Bolotin. He was born on 26th February 1953. He is 69 years old at present. His birthplace is New Haven, Connecticut, the U.S. He started the recording in 1975 at The Church Studio in Tulsa, Oklahoma. His first album was named, after his family name Bolotin. He concentrated on hard rock with his band Blackjack in his early days. He represented Connecticut in the America Song Contest 2022. He was one of the finalists in that show. His last Top 40 single in the US was the hit  "Go the Distance", which got number 1 on the US adult contemporary in the year 1997.

Michael Bolton Career

Michael Bolton had performed in a number of Cameo roles in some movies and TV shows. He took part in those shows as himself. Those shows are Two and a Half Men, The Nanny, and Meet Wally Sparks. He had sung with other artists like  Patti LaBelle, Lucia Aliberti,  Tony Cetinski, José Carreras, Ray Charles, Plácido Domingo, Celine Dion, Renée Fleming, The Lonely Island, Luciano Pavarotti, Wynonna Judd, Zucchero, BB King, and Percy Sledge.  He was one of the contestants on the 11th season of Dancing with the artists. He was eliminated from the dance show along with his dance partner Chelsie Hightower.
