Who Was Serena Karlan? NBA Champion Brian Williams AKA Bison Dele Girlfriend Lost Along With Him

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Brian Williams’s girlfriend, Serena Karlan, is not on the Wikipedia page. 

An American basketball player, Brian Williams, renowned for playing in the NBA, disappeared in mid-2002, along with his girlfriend, Serena Karlan, and Bertrand Saldo, skipper.

In 1997, the player won the NBA championship with the team Bulls. At the age of 30, he retired suddenly, regardless of his beaming basketball career and being the Piston’s highest-paid player; at that time.

Here, let’s look into the girlfriend of the NBA star, who went missing along with him in the 2002 incident.

Who Was Brian Williams’s NBA Girlfriend Serena Karlan? Wikipedia Bio

Serena Karlan was the beloved girlfriend of the NBA star Brian Williams. In 2002, the couple sailed together for their final trip to Auckland but never returned.

Serena does not have a Wikipedia profile; however, her name got featured on the profile of the basketball player. She is mostly- known for her relationship with the player.

She was working in retail and PR but had frustration and unfulfilling jobs. Later on, she settled down in New York City for her work in real estate after getting inspired by her old elementary school teacher.

Further, she reconnected with the player, & that’s when their relationship took a turn. Her mother mentioned, “She had deep connections with people. But Brian was the only one who got her that way.”

What Happened To Bison Dele And Serena Karlan?

Bison Dele, a.k.a, Brian Williams, went sailing for the trip after convincing his girlfriend, Serena Karlan, on 6 July 2002. They made their last satellite phone calls on 8 July 2002.

However, that was the last time anyone heard from the pair following their boat went missing. The case took a turn when a man named Miles Dabord appeared, using the player’s identity.

Miles was an estranged brother of Bison, who had an altercation with him. Dabord’s girlfriend, Erica, at the time, claimed to the authorities that he accidentally killed Serena when they were fighting.

Following the deep investigation, the FBI team strongly believes that  Miles had a connection with their disappearance and possible voyagers’ murder.

Were Serena Karlan And Bison Dele Ever Found?

Serena Karlan and Bison Dele; were never found after they tragically went missing in 2002. Although the officers tried looking for them for years, there was no further news on the pair.

However, their loved ones have kept them in their heart and still recalls them.
